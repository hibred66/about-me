    Q's History

    Born and raised on the Hawaiian island of O'ahu in the city of Honolulu. He was the second oldest of four children.

    Living on O'ahu

    Growing up Q spent almost evey weekend at his grandparents home in Kaimuki/Palolo Valley

    United States Air Force or College? The Choice was made to join the Air Force. Q left for USAF Basic Training in Texas immediately after graduating from High School

    Countries Q lived in

    While in the USAF Q lived in two other countries. His first time overseas was spent on Okinawa, Japan. He lived there for ~ four years. The next move was to the United Kingdom for three years. Then he moved back to Okinawa for another four years.
